How scoring works

Severity (1–5)

  • 1 — Cosmetic only
  • 2 — Minor annoyance
  • 3 — Moderate issue
  • 4 — Major disruption
  • 5 — Serious issue or safety risk

Frequency (1–5)

  • 1 — Rare
  • 2 — Occasional
  • 3 — Regular
  • 4 — Frequent
  • 5 — Constant

Cost score

Calculated automatically based on the dollar cost.

Net score
